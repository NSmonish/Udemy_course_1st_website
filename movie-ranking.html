Must Watch Movies/Series According To Monish

My Top Movies of all time

Avengers: Endgame

The best production by Marvel Studios so far. Nothing will equal the nostalgia you get as a Marvel fan in this movie!

Mission Impossible: Dead Reckoning

If you like Tom Cruise or not, you'll love the action funa nd twists filled in this first part of the film. Not to mention the stunt was performed for real by Tom Cruise!

Klaus

It's a Netflix film and definitly not so famous but it'll sure touch your hearts!

My Top Series of all time

High School Musical: The Musical The Series (HSMTMTS)

If you're a music lover or a theatrical fan then you'll love watching this series each and every bit of it. You can also relate it to the fun and troubles you face in your high school. Watch it to love music more!!

Andor

Well if you're a Star Wars fan you would have watched it already. But this series is not only for them, it symbolises the importance of freedom to a fellow human and the plot is really amazing.

Manifest

Your flight's gonna crash? Well no problemo, you are gonna get second chance to save yourselves! This series is about the higher power who puts a bunch of passengers to test to know about humanity.
